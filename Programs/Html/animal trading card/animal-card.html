Elephant

They're the world's largest land animal.

  • Scientific Name: Loxodonta
  • Number of Species: 3
  • Geographic Home: South East Asia, Siberia, Western Regions
  • Population: 700000(Approx.)

Elephant belong to the largest of the size species. They can reach the length of 6.5 meters and can get as heavy as 6000 kilograms , while also gifted with enough bounding energy to catch their prey. These animals are commonly available in every zoos .
